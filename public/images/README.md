# Section Images

## Required Images

- `about.jpg` — About Me section hero image
- `vc-1.jpg`, `vc-2.jpg` — VC Portfolio items
- `research-1.jpg`, `research-2.jpg` — Research Portfolio items  
- `design-1.jpg`, `design-2.jpg` — Design Portfolio items

## Design Guidelines

- **Style**: Desaturated/monochrome with teal accents
- **Palette**: Match `--bg-0` (#0B0F14) and `--accent-0` (#56D6C7)
- **Format**: JPG or WebP (Next.js will optimize)
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9 or 4:3 recommended
- **Size**: High resolution (Next.js Image handles optimization)

## Inspiration

Match the cinematic, black-and-white aesthetic of [theyearofgreta.com](https://theyearofgreta.com/) with subtle teal overlays.
